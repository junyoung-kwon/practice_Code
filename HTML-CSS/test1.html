Kimchi Stew

Kimchi Stew is a traditional Korean dish that tastes superb.

Ingredients

  • Kimchi
  • Water
  • Spam
  • Pork
  • Veggies

Steps

  1. Step 1: Boil water
  2. Step 2: Put kimchi in boiling water
  3. Step 3: Put Spam, pork, and veggies in boiling water
  4. Step 4: Boil for a couple minutes and serve
